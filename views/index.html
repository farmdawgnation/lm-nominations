Welcome to the Leadership Macon Online Nomination System

Welcome to the online Leadership Macon Nomination System.

This website allows you to quickly and easily submit nominations for next year's Leadership Macon class digitally. You may nominate any number of people for Leadership Macon's class, however please do ensure that you provide accurate contact information for yourself so that we may contact you in the event we have any questions about your nominations.

This online Application is designed to be compatible with IE 9, Firefox 12, Chrome, and Safari. If you experience problems with any of these browsers, then please contact us and let us know. This application may function partially in IE 7 or IE 8, but we do not officially support those browsers.

Thank you for supporting Leadership Macon.
